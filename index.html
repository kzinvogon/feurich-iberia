What Piano Compass is

An accredited home for anyone on a piano-owning journey.

Buying or living with a piano should not mean navigating sales pressure and conflicting opinions alone.

Piano Compass is an independent advisory that stands beside you — not behind a sales counter. We translate a complex market into clear guidance, help you shortlist and play-test the right instruments, arrange independent inspection, and support you for as long as you own the piano. We advise across Feurich, every leading maker, and carefully verified vintage instruments.

Vintage & pre-owned

The right vintage piano can be the buy of a lifetime

A well-chosen vintage instrument offers character, craftsmanship and value that new pianos rarely match — but condition is everything. We source, appraise and independently verify vintage and pre-owned pianos so you buy with certainty, not hope.

  • Provenance and serial-number dating
  • Independent structural & soundboard inspection
  • Honest restoration cost and value forecast
  • Bespoke sourcing for a specific maker or era
