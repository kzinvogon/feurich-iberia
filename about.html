Feurich — Since 1851

Julius Feurich founded his piano manufacturing business in Leipzig in 1851, quickly establishing a reputation for instruments of exceptional tonal quality and mechanical precision. Over five generations, the family advanced the art of piano building without ever compromising the principles on which the company was founded.

Today, Feurich pianos are built in Vienna — the capital of classical music — drawing on the city's unrivalled tradition of instrument making. The Vienna range, particularly the Model 123, is produced entirely in Austria to the highest standards of European craftsmanship.

As an independent piano consultancy, we carry this legacy into every instrument we present, every consultation we offer, and every relationship we build with our clients.
